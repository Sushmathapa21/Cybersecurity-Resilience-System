What is Two-Factor Authentication?

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security to your account. Instead of just using your password, you'll also need a unique 6-digit code from your phone to log in. This makes it much harder for unauthorized users to access your account.

How 2FA Works

Enter Password

Log in with your username and password as usual

Get Code

Open your authenticator app to get a 6-digit code

Enter Code

Enter the code to complete your login

Supported Authenticator Apps

iOS integration

Security Benefits

Protection Against Password Theft

Even if someone gets your password, they can't access your account without the 2FA code.

Real-Time Alerts

You'll know immediately if someone tries to access your account.

Industry Standard Security

Uses the same TOTP technology used by banks and major tech companies.

Easy to Use

Just scan a QR code and you're set up in seconds.

Setup Process

  1. Download an authenticator app from your device's app store (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
  2. Scan the QR code that will appear on the next page with your authenticator app
  3. Enter the 6-digit code from your app to verify the setup
  4. You're all set! Your account is now protected with 2FA
Start 2FA Setup Maybe Later
Important Notes
  • Keep your authenticator app secure and don't share your device
  • Save the backup codes in a secure location
  • If you lose access to your authenticator app, contact support immediately
  • You can disable 2FA anytime from your profile settings
